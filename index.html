Hey there! I'm a UX designer based in Ontario, Canada, and living the international design adventure!

My design journey began as a computer science engineer, ignited by a spark of curiosity and inspiration. I quickly realized the incredible impact of tailoring experiences for users and clients, and it's been a fulfilling ride ever since!

Drawing from my diverse background in crafting and graphic design, I've developed an artful touch in customizing gifts and creating quality artwork and that was my gateway into the world of UX design. Now, that same attention to detail and a user-centric approach fuel my passion for crafting seamless user experiences.

When I'm not immersed in pixels, you can find me savouring engaging conversations over a cup of coffee or exploring my foodie side - can't resist a great meal!
